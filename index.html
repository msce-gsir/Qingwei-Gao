Research interest

I am interested in the unique microstructure of ions, small organic molecule, ionic liquid under nanoconfinement and trying to find out the influence of it on the properties of thermodynamics through molecular simulations. I focus on the transport behavious of nano-confinement fluids，like electrolyte and organic aqueous solution. I have an acquaintance with the Gromacs and LAMMPS package.
